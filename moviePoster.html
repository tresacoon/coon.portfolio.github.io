MOVIE POSTER

Now that our entire world is digital, with Netflix and other online services to stream, we have lost the beauty of going to the movies. With this the art of movie posters have declined. In Abode Illustrator I had the opportunity to make my own movie poster, based on my favorite movie, Safe Haven.

I was faced with the challenge of making segmented images and then attaching them in order with equal distance in Illustrator. This work helped me to learn how to use character text style for the credits section.
