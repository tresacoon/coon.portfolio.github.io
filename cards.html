CARDS

Cards are an easy way to connect personaly and emotionally with others. I have always been interested in making the experience even more personal. In high school I began to make my own paper handmade cards. College has allowed me to further my passion of communication to the digital creation.

My work is inspired by the people around me and who I share these cards with. For the handmade cards I found it challenging to get material and construction. When adhearing the peices you must consider what must go down first. The digital work helped me to better my understanding in Abode InDesign and Illustrator.
