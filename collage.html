COLLAGES

Collages are a way to communicate controversial ideas. As an athlete myself I had a strong opinion about the media representation of women athletes. In college I used my own experiences to reflect women in media. Media did not bring media attention to female atheltes, expect for sexual body representation.

This class was a hands on experience that taught me how to arrange and construct personal works. Physical collage allows the artist to learn how to adhear works together to change the meaning and speak to the audience. I made my work with the intent of bringing attention to female atheletes.

Tresa Coon  ☉  2020
