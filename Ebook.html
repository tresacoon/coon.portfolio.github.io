EBOOK

As the world becomes digital we often move our reading to Ebooks instead of phyiscal books. Using Adobe InDesign I created an Ebook about National Parks, with help from governement national parks websites.

I was interested in creating this work because I love to use color to create a consistent theme and harmony in work. I was also excited to arrange works to communicate with viewers. This works helped me in learning how to use InDesign paragraph styles and how to add animations, slideshows, and other moving elements.

Tresa Coon  ☉  2020
