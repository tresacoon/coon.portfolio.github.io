T-SHIRT DESIGN

A great way to support your school, or promote a company or business is through shirt designs. As a student athlete in high school I had always wanted to create a shirt for my team.

Through Adobe Illustrator I was able to create a unique shirt design for my track team in college. I was challenged by using the pen and shape tools to create a detailed design of the shoe. I enjoyed bringing my own interests into my artwork, with the hope that this will be produced.
