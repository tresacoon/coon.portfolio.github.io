ILLUSTRATION

Illustration are an easy way to communicate with all ages. Illustrator allowed me to create these artworks that use similar shapes and lines to develop an image easily identified by all.

I was faced with a lot of challenges when I first began to work on these images. I had to overcome a learning curve of Adobe Illustor, but picked up on it quickly. I always struggled to hand draw figures, but learned through Illustrator to accomplish figure drawing through shapes.
